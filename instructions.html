How to Use the CyBOK Puzzle App

  1. Go to the Homepage: You'll see five flashcards, each representing a CyBOK Knowledge Group.
  2. Click a Group: This will take you to a page showing the Knowledge Areas (KAs) under that group.
  3. Select a KA: Click any KA card to start its corresponding interactive crossword puzzle.
  4. Solve the Puzzle: Enter answers into the grid using clues provided.
    • Click Check Answers to see which ones are correct.
    • Click Reveal Answers to show the complete solution.
    • Use Reset Puzzle to try again from scratch.
    • Use Final Score to view your completion status and time.
  5. Enjoy Learning: All puzzles are based on official CyBOK content to make learning fun and effective.
